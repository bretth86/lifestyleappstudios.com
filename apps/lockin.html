Accountability Made Social

The motivation of body-doubling, without being in the same room.

1

See Who's Focusing

Your friends list shows who's currently in a focus session. See "Sarah is studying (23 min in)" and feel the pull to join.

2

Join Sessions

Tap to join a friend's session. You'll both see each other focusing. Knowing someone is "with" you makes starting easier.

3

Focus Types

Tag what you're doing: studying, working, creating, coding, reading. See what your friends are working on.

4

Flexible Timer

Default 25-minute Pomodoro, but fully customizable. Some sessions call for 90 minutes of deep work.

5

Daily Stats

Track your focus time and see gentle streaks (that don't punish you for missing days). Celebrate consistency, not perfection.

6

Push Notifications

"Maya just started a study session" - the gentle nudge you need to close TikTok and lock in.

How It Works

Simple accountability in three steps.

1

Add Friends

Share your username or connect through contacts. Build your focus circle.

2

Start a Session

Hit "Lock In", set your focus type and duration. Your friends see you're now focusing.

3

Focus Together

Friends can join your session. You see each other's timers. Social pressure keeps you honest.
