Built for ADHD Brains

Every feature designed around actual ADHD challenges, not neurotypical assumptions.

1

Brain Dump Mode

Open the app and start typing or talking immediately. No categories, no dates, no decisions required. Just dump everything: "call mom", "fix bike", "that thing for work". Organize later (or let AI do it).

2

AI Task Breakdown

Tap any vague task like "clean kitchen" and AI breaks it into small, specific steps under 10 minutes each. No more staring at huge tasks not knowing where to start.

3

Visual Timeline

See your day as colored blocks, not a list. Tasks appear spatially so you can actually feel time passing. Drag to reschedule, tap to start a focus timer.

4

Focus Mode

When you're working, everything else disappears. Just the current task, a gentle countdown, and satisfying animations when you finish. Optional body-doubling sounds to keep you company.

5

No Streak Punishment

"You did 3/5 tasks today" - not "You broke your 47-day streak". We celebrate what you accomplish, not what you miss. Because ADHD brains don't need more guilt.

6

Voice Input

Capture thoughts the moment they appear. Speak into your phone and watch tasks appear. Perfect for when typing feels like too much.
