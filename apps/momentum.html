The Momentum Way

Designed around human psychology, not gamification.

1

Weekly Goals, Not Daily Streaks

Set "exercise 5x per week" not "exercise every day". Life happens. Flexibility is built in.

2

Momentum Curves

See 4-week trends, not streak counters. Your momentum goes up and down naturally. A dip isn't a failure, it's just a dip.

3

Celebrate Partial Success

Hit 4 out of 5 days? "Great week!" not "You failed once." We measure what you did, not what you missed.

4

Week-at-a-Glance

Visual display of your week. See habits as colored dots, quickly tap to mark complete. Simple and satisfying.

5

Weekly Reflections

Sunday prompt: "How did this week go?" Add notes about what worked, what didn't. Build self-awareness over time.

6

Trend Charts

See your consistency over months. Long-term trends matter more than individual days. Zoom out and see progress.
