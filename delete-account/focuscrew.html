Delete Your FocusCrew Account

Data Deletion Request

We respect your right to control your data. You can delete your FocusCrew account and all associated data using one of the methods below.

Option 1: Delete from the App (Recommended)

The fastest way to delete your account is directly within the FocusCrew app:

  1. Open the FocusCrew app
  2. Go to Settings (gear icon)
  3. Scroll down to the Account section
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm deletion when prompted

Your account and all data will be deleted immediately.

Option 2: Request Deletion via Email

If you cannot access the app or prefer to request deletion via email:

Request Account Deletion

Please include the email address associated with your FocusCrew account. We will process your request within 7 days and send confirmation once complete.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

When you delete your account, we permanently remove:

  • Profile Information: Your email, display name, and profile photo
  • Focus Sessions: All your recorded focus session history
  • Statistics: Your streaks, total focus time, and daily stats
  • Friend Connections: All friendships and pending requests

What Happens After Deletion?

  • Your account cannot be recovered after deletion
  • Your friends will no longer see you in their friends list
  • Any active sessions will be terminated
  • If you had a Pro subscription, it will be cancelled (manage via App Store/Google Play)

Data Retention

Once deletion is confirmed:

  • All personal data is removed from our servers immediately
  • Backup data is purged within 30 days
  • Anonymized, aggregated analytics may be retained
