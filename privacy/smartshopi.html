Privacy Policy for SmartShopi

Last Updated: February 23, 2024

Lifestyle App Studios operates the SmartShopi mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

Our Privacy Commitment

SmartShopi helps you compare grocery prices by scanning recipes. We use AI to extract ingredients but do not store your recipe images.

Information We Collect

Recipe Photos

When you scan a recipe:

  • Photo is sent to OCR service for text extraction
  • Photo is immediately deleted after processing
  • Extracted ingredients are stored locally
  • We do not keep copies of your photos

Shopping Lists

  • Ingredients you save
  • Price comparisons you view
  • Your shopping history

This data is stored locally on your device.

Location (Optional)

If you enable location:

  • Used to find nearby supermarkets
  • Never stored or transmitted to our servers
  • Can be disabled anytime

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Account information (no signup)
  • Payment information
  • Precise purchase history

How We Use Your Information

  • Recipe Scanning: Extract ingredients from photos
  • Price Comparison: Show prices at local stores
  • Shopping Lists: Help you organize ingredients

Data Storage

  • Local Storage: Shopping lists on your device
  • No Cloud Sync: Data stays on your phone
  • No Account: Use immediately without signup

Your Rights

  • Delete all shopping lists
  • Clear scan history
  • Disable location access
  • Uninstall to remove all data

Children's Privacy

SmartShopi is suitable for all ages and collects no personal information.
