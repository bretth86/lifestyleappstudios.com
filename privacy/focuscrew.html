Privacy Policy for FocusCrew

Last Updated: February 23, 2024

Lifestyle App Studio operates the FocusCrew mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Our Privacy Commitment

FocusCrew requires some cloud features to enable social focus sessions with friends. We collect only what's necessary and never sell your data.

Information We Collect

Account Information

To use social features, you create an account with:

  • Email address (for login)
  • Display name (shown to friends)
  • Profile photo (optional)

Focus Session Data

  • Session start/end times
  • Session duration and streaks
  • Friends you connect with

This data enables the social features - seeing when friends are studying and joining sessions together.

Advertising Data

For free users, we display ads through Google AdMob. You can upgrade to Pro to remove ads.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Location data
  • Contacts (we don't auto-import)
  • Screen content or browsing history
  • Payment details (handled by App Store/Play Store)

How We Use Your Information

  • Social Features: Show your focus status to friends
  • Analytics: Display your personal focus statistics
  • Display Ads: Show advertisements to free users
  • Process Purchases: Verify Pro status

Data Storage

  • Cloud Storage: Account and session data stored securely on Supabase
  • Local Cache: Recent sessions cached for offline viewing
  • Encryption: All data transmitted over HTTPS

Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete your account and all data from Settings
  • Export your focus data
  • Remove individual friends

Children's Privacy

FocusCrew is intended for users 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
