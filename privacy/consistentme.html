Privacy Policy for ConsistentMe

Last Updated: February 23, 2024

100% Offline: ConsistentMe works entirely offline. Your habits, completions, and momentum data never leave your device.

Lifestyle App Studios operates the ConsistentMe mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains our commitment to your privacy.

Our Privacy Commitment

ConsistentMe is designed with privacy as a core feature. We believe your habit data is personal and should stay that way.

Information We Collect

We Collect NOTHING

ConsistentMe operates entirely on your device. We have no servers, no analytics, and no way to access your data.

Data Stored Locally

The following exists only on your device:

  • Your habits and goals
  • Daily completions
  • Momentum charts and statistics
  • Weekly reflections
  • App preferences

Purchase Information

Premium purchases are handled by Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We only receive confirmation of purchase status.

No Network Connections

ConsistentMe does not:

  • Connect to the internet
  • Send analytics or crash reports
  • Display advertisements
  • Sync to any cloud service
  • Require an account

Data Storage

  • Local SQLite Database: All data encrypted on device
  • No Cloud Backup: Use your device's backup for data preservation
  • Complete Control: You own your data entirely

Third-Party Services

ServicePurposeData Shared
App Store / Play StorePurchases onlyTransaction confirmation
No other third-party services

Your Rights

Since all data is local:

  • Clear all data from Settings
  • Uninstall to delete everything
  • Export data for personal backup

Children's Privacy

ConsistentMe is suitable for all ages (4+) and collects no personal information.
