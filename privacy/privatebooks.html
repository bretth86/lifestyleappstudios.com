Privacy Policy for PrivateBooks

Last Updated: February 23, 2024

Privacy-First Design: PrivateBooks is designed to work 100% offline. Your financial data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to any server.

Lifestyle App Studios operates the PrivateBooks mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains our commitment to your privacy.

Our Privacy Commitment

PrivateBooks is built on a simple principle: your financial data is yours alone. We have no servers, no accounts, and no way to access your information.

Information We Collect

We Collect NOTHING

PrivateBooks operates entirely offline. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your data. Period.

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data exists only on your device:

  • Transactions and categories
  • Account balances
  • Budgets and goals
  • Receipt photos
  • Reports and analytics

Purchase Information

If you purchase Pro features, the transaction is handled entirely by Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We receive only a confirmation of your purchase status.

No Network Connections

PrivateBooks does not:

  • Connect to the internet for core functionality
  • Sync to any cloud service
  • Send analytics or crash reports
  • Display advertisements
  • Connect to your bank accounts

Data Export

You can export your data as CSV or JSON files. These exports are saved to your device and are under your complete control.

Third-Party Services

ServicePurposeData Shared
App Store / Play StorePurchases onlyTransaction confirmation
No other third-party services

Data Security

  • All data stored in encrypted SQLite database
  • Protected by your device's security (PIN, Face ID, etc.)
  • Never transmitted over any network

Your Rights

Since all data is local:

  • Delete everything by uninstalling the app
  • Export your data anytime
  • You have complete control

Children's Privacy

PrivateBooks does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children.
