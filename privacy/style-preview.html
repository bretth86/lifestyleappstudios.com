Privacy Policy for Style Preview

Last Updated: February 23, 2024

Lifestyle App Studios operates the Style Preview mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Our Privacy Commitment

Style Preview uses AI to transform your photos. We process your images securely and do not store them on our servers after transformation.

Information We Collect

Photos You Provide

When you use Style Preview, you upload selfies for AI transformation. These photos are:

  • Sent to our AI service for processing
  • Automatically deleted from servers after transformation
  • Cached locally on your device
  • Never used for training AI models

Transformed Images

The AI-generated transformations are:

  • Returned to your device
  • Stored locally in your gallery
  • Not retained on our servers

Purchase Information

Premium purchases are handled by Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We only receive purchase confirmation.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Your name or email (no account required)
  • Location data
  • Contacts or browsing history
  • Biometric data

How We Use Your Information

  • AI Transformation: Process your photos to generate style previews
  • Process Purchases: Verify Premium status

Data Storage

  • Local Storage: Transformations saved on your device
  • No Cloud Storage: We don't keep copies of your photos
  • Secure Transmission: All uploads use HTTPS encryption

Photo Retention

Important: Your photos are processed in real-time and automatically deleted from our AI provider's servers. We do not maintain any database of user photos.

Your Rights

  • Delete local transformations anytime
  • Clear app cache from Settings
  • Uninstall to remove all local data

Children's Privacy

Style Preview is intended for users 13 and older. We do not knowingly process photos of children under 13.
