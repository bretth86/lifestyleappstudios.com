Privacy Policy for TaskBeGone

Last Updated: February 23, 2024

Offline-First Design: TaskBeGone stores all your tasks locally on your device. AI features use Google Gemini but your task content is never stored on external servers.

Lifestyle App Studios operates the TaskBeGone mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

Our Privacy Commitment

TaskBeGone is built for people with ADHD who need a task manager that respects their privacy. Your tasks and brain dumps stay on your device.

Information We Collect

Task Data (Local Only)

All your tasks, brain dumps, and timelines are stored locally:

  • Task names and descriptions
  • Due dates and priorities
  • Brain dump entries
  • Completed task history

This data never leaves your device unless you use AI breakdown.

AI Task Breakdown

When you use AI to break down tasks:

  • Task text is sent to Google Gemini
  • Processing happens in real-time
  • Content is not stored by Google for training
  • Results are saved locally on your device

Voice Input

Speech-to-text is processed on-device using your phone's built-in capabilities. Audio is not transmitted to our servers.

Purchase Information

Purchases are handled by Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We only receive purchase confirmation.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Account information (no signup required)
  • Location data
  • Contacts or calendar access
  • Usage analytics

Data Storage

  • Local Database: SQLite database on your device
  • No Cloud Sync: Data stays on your device (Sync+ coming soon)
  • No Account: Use immediately without signup

Your Rights

  • Delete all data from Settings

Children's Privacy

TaskBeGone is suitable for all ages and does not collect personal information.
