Privacy Policy for CrewVote

Last Updated: February 23, 2024

Lifestyle App Studios operates the CrewVote mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

Our Privacy Commitment

CrewVote helps groups decide on activities together. We collect minimal information needed for the social features to work.

Information We Collect

Account Information

To use CrewVote, you provide:

  • Display name (shown to your group)
  • Optional profile photo
  • Group memberships

Voting Data

  • Your swipes/votes on activities
  • Groups you create or join
  • Activity suggestions you add

This data enables the matching feature to find activities everyone agrees on.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Email or phone number
  • Location data
  • Contacts (we don't import)
  • Payment information (app is free)

How We Use Your Information

  • Group Matching: Find activities everyone likes
  • Social Features: Connect with your group members

Data Storage

  • Cloud Storage: Group data stored on secure servers
  • Minimal Data: We only store what's needed for features
  • Encryption: All data transmitted via HTTPS

Third-Party Services

ServicePurposeData Shared
SupabaseBackend & StorageAccount, groups, votes

Your Rights

  • Delete your account and all data
  • Leave any group
  • Remove your voting history

Children's Privacy

CrewVote is intended for users 13 and older.
