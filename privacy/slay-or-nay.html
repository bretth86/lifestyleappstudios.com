Privacy Policy for Slay or Nay

Last Updated: February 23, 2024

Lifestyle App Studio ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Slay or Nay mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Our Privacy Commitment

We believe in Privacy First. Slay or Nay is designed to keep your data on your device whenever possible. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Information We Collect

Photos You Provide

When you use Slay or Nay to rate an outfit, you provide photos from your camera or photo library. These photos are:

  • Sent to our AI service (Google Gemini) for analysis
  • Not stored on our servers after processing
  • Cached locally on your device for your rating history
  • Under your control to delete at any time

Purchase Information

If you purchase Premium, the transaction is handled entirely by Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We receive only a confirmation of your purchase status, not your payment details.

Advertising Data

For free users, we display ads through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and ad interaction data. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Your name, email, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Contacts or address book
  • Browsing history

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide the Service: Process your outfit photos through AI to generate ratings
  • Display Ads: Show advertisements to free users
  • Process Purchases: Verify Premium subscription status

Data Storage

  • Local Storage: Your rating history is stored locally on your device
  • No Account Required: We don't require personal information
  • No Cloud Sync: Your data stays on your device

Your Rights

You can delete all your local data by going to Settings > Clear All Data, or by uninstalling the app.

Children's Privacy

Slay or Nay is rated for ages 4+ and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
